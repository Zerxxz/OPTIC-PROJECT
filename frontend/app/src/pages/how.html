STEP 01
23| 23|

Strategy commitment

24| 24|

The owner uploads a strategy to Walrus and the agent stores the 25| 25| blob_id + sha256 on-chain. The Move contract refuses to act 26| 26| on any strategy that doesn't match the committed hash.

27| 27|
    28| 28|
  • Walrus blob: strategy code + parameters
    • 29| 29|
  • On-chain: blob_id, sha256, suins_name
    • 30| 30|
  • Refusal: enum mismatch → abort

STEP 02
35| 35|

Capability-gated actions

36| 36|

Three roles — quant, risk, executor — each get 37| 37| a AgentCap. The Move contract enforces that an order can only be placed 38| 38| when the right combination of caps sign the PTB.

39| 39|
    40| 40|
  • quant_cap: 0 — propose trades
    • 41| 41|
  • risk_cap: 1 — propose hedges, veto
    • 42| 42|
  • exec_cap: 2 — dispatch PTB
    • 43| 43|

STEP 03
47| 47|

Multi-agent decision

48| 48|

Off-chain, a Quant, Risk, and Executor 49| 49| specialist negotiate. Risk can veto. The winning decision — with full reasoning — is 50| 50| logged to Walrus and anchored to the on-chain audit entry.

51| 51|
    52| 52|
  • Inputs: Agent state, market state, strategy
    • 53| 53|
  • Merge: risk veto, else highest confidence
    • 54| 54|
  • Output: Decision{action, reasoning, confidenceBps}
    • 55| 55|

STEP 04
59| 59|

Predict hedge

60| 60|

When realized vol crosses the threshold, RiskAgent opens a NO 61| 61| position on DeepBook Predict as a tail-risk hedge. The strike is 62| 62| computed from spot mid and capped at 1% of treasury.

63| 63|
    64| 64|
  • Threshold: volBps > 800 (8%)
    • 65| 65|
  • Strike: 5% OTM put from mid
    • 66| 66|
  • Size: 1% of treasury balance

STEP 05
71| 71|

Strategy NFT via Kiosk

72| 72|

The strategy blob is published to Sui Kiosk as a transferrable 73| 73| NFT. Sell, license, or fork any strategy on a public marketplace.
