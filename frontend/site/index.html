How it works

1

Strategy commitment

The owner uploads a strategy to Walrus and the agent stores the blob_id + sha256 on-chain. The Move contract refuses to act on any strategy that doesn't match the committed hash.

2

Capability-gated actions

Three roles — quant, risk, executor — each get a AgentCap. The Move contract enforces that an order can only be placed when the right combination of caps sign the PTB.

3

Multi-agent decision

Off-chain, a Quant, Risk, and Executor specialist negotiate. Risk can veto. The winning decision — with full reasoning — is logged to Walrus and anchored to the on-chain audit entry.

4

Predict hedge

When realized vol crosses the threshold, RiskAgent opens a NO position on DeepBook Predict as a tail-risk hedge. The strike is computed from spot mid and capped at 1% of treasury.

5

Strategy NFT via Kiosk

The strategy blob is published to Sui Kiosk as a transferrable NFT. Sell, license, or fork any strategy on a public marketplace.

6

Realtime transparency

This very page reads the audit log straight from Walrus and renders the latest decisions. No backend, no scraping, no trust.

The three specialist agents

quant.sui

Quant

Reads the DeepBook orderbook + indexer, evaluates a pluggable strategy (mean-reversion, momentum, market-making, or custom), and proposes a trade with a confidence score.

  • Inputs: Agent state, market state, strategy blob
  • Output: place_order or no_op
  • Confidence: 0–10,000 bps
risk.sui

Risk

Monitors realized vol, drawdown, and exposure. Has veto power: can pause the agent entirely or open a DeepBook Predict hedge to cap downside.

  • Circuit breaker: -$50 daily loss → pause
  • High vol: > 8% → open NO hedge (1% of treasury)
  • Strike: 5% OTM put, 24h expiry
executor.sui

Executor

Defence-in-depth gate. Re-checks every risk param, sizes within exposure limits, and composes the final PTB. Logs an AuditEntry to Walrus.

  • Defence in depth — never trust upstream
  • Idempotent dispatch
  • Audit log on every action
